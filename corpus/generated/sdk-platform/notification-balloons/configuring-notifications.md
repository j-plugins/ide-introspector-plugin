Notifications are configured in `Settings | Appearance & Behavior | Notifications`.
Observe the Bagel notification group with Popup type set to Balloon and Show in tool window being checked.

See [Notifications Product Help](https://www.jetbrains.com/help/idea/notifications.html#notifications_tool_window) for specific user-facing options.
